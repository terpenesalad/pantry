Pantry
a little guide
Pantry
Track items by name, category, qty, location, stock level and expiry. Quick-add from search. Swipe left to delete. ＋ sends to shopping list. Categories are draggable. 🔔 Reminder downloads a .ics calendar file with an alert. Today's planned meal shows at the top — tap View or Cook.
Shopping List
Out-of-stock items auto-appear. + Low Stock bulk-adds. Recipes add missing ingredients only. Maybe holds treats separately. Tick off as you shop.
Recipes
Sorted by what you can cook now. Filter by Can Make, In Season, or cuisine. Cook mode: tick ingredients, scale servings, step-by-step with auto-timers. Cooked It deducts pantry. Header: ⏱ Timer, ⇄ Convert, ✦ Suggest, 📅 Meal Planner.
Meal Planner
Plan meals day by day. Assign leftovers from the previous day. Copy last week in one tap. Auto-generate a shopping list for missing ingredients. Navigate past/future weeks. Tap 📊 for cooking stats.
Bar
Same as Recipes but for cocktails — method, glassware, garnish, difficulty, photo. Pantry-aware, guided mode, drag to reorder.
Self
Hydration tracker with daily goal. Meal check-ins (Breakfast / Lunch / Dinner) — tracked silently for future analytics. Meditation with breathing + binaural beats. In Season Melbourne produce. Wordle, Words with Grease, Coin flip, Radio.
Map
Pin places across Victoria. Search any address. 🌙 for dark tiles. Pins sync live.
✦ Suggest
AI recipe + cocktail ideas cross-referenced with your pantry. Cached locally. Message Kami for an API key, then tap ⚙ Key to enter it.
Backup
Top right of Pantry. Download everything as a JSON file and restore any time. Do it occasionally.
